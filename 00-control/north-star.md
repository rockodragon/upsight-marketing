## Mission
UpSight is the conversational intelligence and customer discovery platform that turns every customer conversation into structured, evidence-backed insight.

## Positioning
**Receipts for every customer insight.** Chief-of-staff vibe for distributed teams.
Not a transcription tool. Not single-call LLM analysis. A memory/accumulation layer.

## ICP (current hypothesis — refresh from `20-research/voice-of-customer/icp.md`)
- Founders / heads of product running active customer discovery
- Distributed teams that need shared customer-truth
- Companies experiencing "Apollo fatigue" or generic-CRM frustration

## Q2 2026 goals (write 3, no more)
1. **3 paying customers** — at least 2 from the consultant/founder beachhead. Cytodyme is #1. Unlock angel pitching.
2. **$300 MRR** — proves willingness to pay. One Starter + two trials converting = done.
3. **Desktop recording reliability shipped** — Recall.ai mic-drop pattern fixed and hardened. Unblocks every demo.

## Anti-goals (what we're explicitly not doing)
- Becoming a generic CRM
- Competing on transcription quality
- Enterprise sales motion before product-led signal works
