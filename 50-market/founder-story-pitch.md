## The 3-Sentence Slide

Rick Moy is a multi-exit founder and B2B SaaS operator with 20+ years building category-defining companies in cybersecurity and AI. As co-founder and CEO of NSS Labs, he created an entirely new market — empirical security testing — by discovering that enterprise buyers were spending millions with zero verifiable data. UpSight is the same bet applied to product teams: teams are making major product decisions every week with no reliable evidence, and Rick is building the infrastructure to change that.

## The Conversation Version

### The Origin

NSS Labs started because Rick kept watching the same scene: large enterprises making $1M+ security purchasing decisions based on vendor marketing, analyst opinions, and gut feel — with no independent, empirical evidence. He discovered this by actually talking to hundreds of IT security professionals. They wanted proof. Nobody was providing it.

He built NSS Labs to provide it. It became the definitive source of security product performance data. That's a market-creation story, not a feature-shipping story.

**The pattern:** find a domain where decisions are high-stakes and evidence is absent → build the system that creates verifiable evidence → the market organizes around it.

UpSight is running the same play. The domain is product decisions. The buyers are founders and PMs. The stakes are lower (runway vs. enterprise budgets) but the frequency is higher and the market is much larger.

## The "Why Now" Connector

The AI piece makes this a 2024-2026 story, not a 2018 story:

1. **LLMs made structured extraction economically viable.** Running evidence extraction on 1,000 customer conversations now costs $5-15. In 2021, it wasn't possible. This is what unlocks the product.

2. **AI coding tools shifted the bottleneck upstream.** Cursor and Claude Code made building software fast. But building the *right* software? That's now the expensive constraint. YC called this out explicitly in their Spring 2026 RFS: "Cursor for PMs" is their #1 wanted startup idea. UpSight is that product.

3. **MCP makes UpSight a platform, not a feature.** Model Context Protocol means AI agents can query UpSight's evidence layer directly. UpSight isn't just a web app — it's infrastructure for the AI-native product team. That distribution surface didn't exist 18 months ago.

---

## The "Why Not Dovetail / VoicePanel / Gong" Connector

- Dovetail is for dedicated UX research teams. Most UpSight users don't have one.
- VoicePanel is AI-only interviews. Rick's insight: the highest-stakes conversations (stakeholder alignment, board meetings, sales calls) need human judgment, not AI autopilot.
- Gong is $66K/year minimum and built for sales coaching, not product decisions.

Rick's wedge: "I built NSS Labs for the $100K+ buyer with procurement. UpSight is for the $29-79/mo buyer who puts it on their personal card. The market is 100x larger."
