## Pre-Recording Checklist

Inherited and updated from the SSD demo script.

### Project state
- [ ] Demo project has 4–6 processed interviews (mix of sales + discovery flavor)
- [ ] Themes view shows 3+ themes, each with 3+ evidence quotes
- [ ] At least one theme has quotes from 4+ distinct speakers (for Scene 5 click-through)
- [ ] Lens dropdown has all 4 lenses configured (Sales & BD · Product Discovery · Churn Risk · Consulting / SOW)
- [ ] Project name is clean (no "Test Project" / "asdf")

### In-Call Guidance (Scene 6) — requires reliability
- [ ] Desktop app installed and tested with a 2-minute test recording in last 24h
- [ ] Research goals are set on the demo project — Guidance surfaces them
- [ ] Confirm Guidance panel renders side-by-side without latency
- [ ] Have a **fallback recording** of the Guidance panel working in case it fails live

### Follow-Up Draft (Scene 7) — requires reliability
- [ ] Confirm post-meeting draft generation is working end-to-end on demo project
- [ ] Pre-test once with a 5-minute test call to make sure the draft contains evidence links
- [ ] Have a **screenshot of a working draft** as fallback

### Production
- [ ] Record screen first, silent (ScreenStudio or QuickTime)
- [ ] Narrate in post (Descript)
- [ ] Captions on by default in final export
- [ ] Final formats: 1920×1080 master · 1080×1080 LinkedIn cutdown · 1080×1920 social cutdown

## Risk / Contingency

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Trigger.dev / processing pipeline broken → no evidence on new recording | Medium | Use pre-seeded interviews for Scenes 4–5; only show the act of recording in Scene 2 |
| Desktop app crashes mid-record | Low | Backup recording from yesterday's test |
| In-Call Guidance panel doesn't load | **Medium-high — new feature** | Pre-recorded clip of Guidance panel in action; cut to it |
| Follow-up draft isn't generated, or is generic/wrong | **Medium-high — new feature** | Pre-tested draft from yesterday; screenshot it and zoom into key sections |
| Live transcript lags → looks broken on camera | Medium | Skip Scene 2's live aspect; show transcript already attached to the recording |
| Lens dropdown doesn't visually re-organize the themes view | Low | Cut between two screen recordings — one with each lens — instead of live swap |

## Voiceover Notes

- **Pace:** 120 wpm. Confident, not rushed. Operator archetype (§8.1 Trusted Operator) — measured, mid-register.
- **Don't explain the UI — narrate the value.** If you find yourself saying "you can click here," cut it.
- **Stage transitions get a half-beat of silence** so on-screen stage labels (`// 01 capture`, `// 02 understand`, `// 03 act`) read.
- **End on the tagline.** Don't add a "thanks for watching" tail — the tagline is the close.
- **Phrases to keep** (brand-aligned, §8.3 We Say):
  - "Specific claims, with the exact words behind them" (Receipts moment, demoted from lead per §13)
  - "Your prompts, surfaced in the moment"
  - "Before you close the laptop, the follow-up's drafted"
  - "Get your customers. Build conviction."
- **Phrases to never use** (§8.3 We Don't Say): "Revolutionary," "AI-powered," "Leverage voice-of-customer," "Actionable intelligence," "10x"

## Persona-Specific Cutdowns (derive from this master)

This is how you produce per-persona variants without re-shooting. **The Lens swap (Scene 5) and the closing line are the only changes.**

| Persona | Scene 5 lens to land on | Scene 8 closing tagline | Use case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sales / BD** | Sales & BD lens — themes show as objections, buying signals, competitor mentions | "Never walk into a call unprepared. Never let a deal go cold." | Sales landing page · Sales-team intro video |
| **Consultant** | Consulting / SOW lens — themes show as stakeholder agreement, scope risks | "From stakeholder interviews to undeniable recommendations." | Consultant landing page · Beachhead outreach |
| **Founder** | Product Discovery lens — themes show as jobs-to-be-done, feature requests | "Know every customer. Build the right thing. Never drop the ball." | Homepage variant · Founder community posts |
| **Post-Launch Founder** | Land on cross-conversation synthesis emphasis | "Your customers already told you what's working. Hear them." | "I have 20 calls — what now?" landing page |
| **Product Lead** | Product Discovery lens, but emphasize Themes-to-backlog | "Research that ships." | Product community channels |

For all of these, Scenes 1–4 + 6–7 stay identical. **Single shoot, five outputs.** That's the win of a horizontal demo with a configurable lens.

## Length Cutdowns

| Length | Use case | What to cut |
|---|---|---|
| **2:00** | Sales-call follow-up · embedded in pricing page | Drop Scene 3 (Capture: every source) and trim Scene 5 to one lens |
| **1:30** | LinkedIn / homepage hero | Compress Capture to 0:15 total · keep both Act scenes intact (the differentiator) |
| **0:60** | Paid social retargeting | Hook (10s) → Act triptych (40s) → CTA (10s) |
| **0:30** | Paid social cold | Guidance + Follow-up draft only · CTA |
| **0:06** | YouTube bumper | Logo + tagline + one Act moment |

**Rule of thumb:** the Act scenes (6–7) are non-negotiable in any cutdown longer than :30. They're the differentiator. Everything else is optional.
