# Walkthrough — Discover Path (Upload → Evidence → Receipts)

> **Maps to:** Steps-to-Wow **Blue Path** (user-success-journeys §6) · Activation Path A: Conversation Path
> **Primary personas served:** Consultant (beachhead), Founder, Post-Launch Founder
> **Aligned to:** Brand Brief v4.0 · Persona Flow Matrix v1.1
> **Target length:** 2:30–3:00 · ~360 words · moderate pace
> **Format:** 1920×1080 landscape · captions on by default · YouTube + in-app empty state + Day 1 onboarding email

---

## Creative Brief

| | |
|---|---|
| **Job** | Move signed-up users past the "first upload" stall point (user-success-journeys §5 Interventions). |
| **Audience** | Signed up, hasn't uploaded a conversation yet — OR uploaded but hasn't viewed results. |
| **One thing to believe** | "I can go from recording to grounded insight today — not in two weeks." |
| **Voice** | Clear, helpful, action-oriented (Brand Brief §8.4 "Product UI" + "Success moments"). Fewer adjectives, more verbs. |
| **Success metric** | % of viewers who return to app and complete `encounters > 0` within 24h. |

---

## SCRIPT

### CHAPTER 1 — "Here's what you're going to see" (0:00–0:15)
**Visual:** Clean full-screen title card. UpSight mark + chapter headline.
**On-screen:** **"From upload to evidence in 3 steps."**
**VO:**
> "Three steps. About two minutes. You'll upload a conversation, see what UpSight found in it, and click through to the exact moments your customers said it. Let's go."

---

### CHAPTER 2 — STEP 1: Start a project (0:15–0:35)
**Visual:** Screen recording. Logged into UpSight. Click "+ New Project." Voice-first setup prompts: *"What are you trying to learn?"* User types: *"Why enterprise prospects stall after demo."* Project created.
**On-screen callout (mono):** `// context shapes the analysis`
**VO:**
> "Start with a project. One line on what you're trying to learn. This is the context UpSight uses to decide what matters in every conversation you add — so it isn't just summarizing, it's listening *for* something."

**Production note:** Pre-fill the prompt for consistency. Don't burn time on typing.

---

### CHAPTER 3 — STEP 2: Add a conversation (0:35–1:15)
**Visual:** Show the three add-paths, then use Upload for this walkthrough.
1. Quick pan over: **Upload file** · **Record live** · **Paste transcript**.
2. Drag-and-drop a `.mp4` of a recorded sales/discovery call into the dropzone.
3. Upload bar. Transcription spinner — *jump cut* so it feels instant.
4. Status: "Analyzed."

**On-screen callouts:**
- `// works with Zoom, Meet, Loom, phone`
- `// transcription + evidence extraction`

**VO:**
> "Now add a conversation. Upload a recording. Record live in the meeting. Or paste a transcript you already have. UpSight handles the rest — transcription, speaker attribution, and evidence extraction against your project goal. For this one, I'll drag in a discovery call I did yesterday."

**Production note:** Use a real (anonymized) call. Fake ones read as fake on camera.

---

### CHAPTER 4 — STEP 3: See what UpSight found (1:15–2:00)
**Visual:**
1. Navigate to the conversation view. Evidence cards are stacked — each with a timestamped quote, speaker name, and a tag (objection, buying signal, pain point, feature request).
2. Hover one card — it highlights the transcript line.
3. Click to the **Themes** tab. Three themes visible with evidence counts: *"Procurement slows us down (4 quotes)"*, *"Worried about data residency (3 quotes)"*, *"Wants Slack integration (2 quotes)."*

**On-screen callouts:**
- `// every finding links to a moment`
- `// themes group across conversations`

**VO:**
> "Here's what UpSight pulled out. Every card is a specific moment from the call — a pain point, an objection, a buying signal — with the timestamp attached. Flip to Themes, and you see what clustered. One call, three emerging patterns, each with the receipts right there."

---

### CHAPTER 5 — THE RECEIPTS MOMENT (2:00–2:25)
**Visual:** Click one theme → evidence drawer slides open. Four exact quotes from different speakers, each with a "Play from 4:23" button. Press play — audio clip plays for 3 seconds. The speaker's actual words. Stop.
**On-screen headline:** **"This is what grounded looks like."**
**VO:**
> "Click any finding, and you're one step from the source. Their words, their voice, the exact moment. No AI hallucination. No 'the model said so.' When your team asks — *or your client, or your board* — the evidence is right there."

**Brand brief alignment:** §3.2 "Every insight is grounded. Every action is informed."

---

### CHAPTER 6 — WHAT'S NEXT (2:25–2:45)
**Visual:** Back to dashboard. Two soft prompts glow in the assistant panel:
- *"Draft a follow-up email from this call"*
- *"Add another conversation to build the pattern"*

**VO:**
> "From here, two moves. Let an agent draft the follow-up while the call is fresh. Or add two more conversations and watch the pattern sharpen. Five conversations is the number where things really come together."

---

### CHAPTER 7 — CTA (2:45–3:00)
**Visual:** UpSight logo. Amber CTA: *"Upload your first conversation."*
**VO:**
> "Your turn. Upload one conversation. See what your customers have been telling you."

**Super:** `getupsight.com/start`

## Production Notes

- **Record in real app, not mockups.** Receipts moment loses 90% of its power if it's faked.
- **Use an anonymized but real discovery/sales call.** The quotes need to sound like real customers.
- **Jump-cut transcription spinner.** Real processing time isn't the story — the result is.
- **Captions on by default** — walkthroughs get watched muted in-app.
- **Chapter markers in YouTube upload** so viewers can jump to Step 2 or Step 3 directly.
