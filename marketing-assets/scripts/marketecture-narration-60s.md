# Marketecture Narration — 60s

> **Source slide:** `../../50-market/upsight-marketecture.html`
> **Format:** Voiceover over screen capture of the marketecture slide. Click-advance triggers each reveal beat — narration syncs to clicks.
> **Length:** ~60s · ~140 words · 140 wpm
> **Use:** Investor decks · sales-call openers · LinkedIn explainer · embedded on About / How-It-Works page

---

## Production approach

1. Open `upsight-marketecture.html` in browser, full-screen
2. Record screen capture (press space/click to advance through 4 reveal states)
3. Narrate in post — pace clicks to land with each VO beat below
4. Leave 0.5s breath between beats so reveals visually settle

---

## Script (4 beats, one per reveal)

### Beat 0 → 1 · Reveal CAPTURE (≈ 0:00–0:15)
**On click:** Capture stage fades in (channels list + mode chips + meeting bots).

> "UpSight turns every customer conversation into intelligence your whole team can act on. It works in three stages.
>
> **Capture.** Calls, surveys, notes, events. Live recording, voice agent, upload, kiosk. Joins Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, GoTo. However you talk to customers — UpSight is there."

---

### Beat 1 → 2 · Reveal UNDERSTAND (≈ 0:15–0:30)
**On click:** Understand stage fades in (role/company/segment · themes · cross-channel patterns · lenses).

> "**Understand.** Every conversation sliced by who said it — role, company, segment. Clustered into themes, with the receipts attached. Connected across channels. Configurable lenses tune what UpSight watches for — sales, discovery, churn, consulting."

---

### Beat 2 → 3 · Reveal ACT (≈ 0:30–0:45)
**On click:** Act stage fades in (Guidance · Follow-Up Drafts · Priorities · Deal Intel).

> "**Act.** Your prompts surface as in-call guidance. Follow-up emails draft themselves. Priorities and deal intelligence stay current — automatically. Take the next step, or let an agent take it for you."

---

### Beat 3 → 4 · Reveal PLATFORM CORE (≈ 0:45–1:00)
**On click:** Platform Core fades in (Agentic CRM · Knowledge Base · Prioritization Engine · Open via MCP with Claude/ChatGPT/OpenClaw chips).

> "All of it runs on one core. An agentic CRM. Your customer knowledge base. A prioritization engine. And an open MCP server — so Claude, ChatGPT, and OpenClaw plug in directly.
>
> Get your customers. Build conviction."

---

## Voice constraints (Brand Brief §8)

- **Confident, measured.** Trusted Operator. ~140 wpm.
- **No** "revolutionary," "AI-powered," "10x," "leverage," "actionable intelligence."
- **Land the tagline last.** No tail after "Build conviction."
