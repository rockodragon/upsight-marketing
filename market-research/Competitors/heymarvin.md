## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **Speed/efficiency** — "Cut your research time in half," "Raw data to insights in minutes," "Field Nation saved 20+ hours analyzing 100+ interviews"
2. **Scale without headcount** — "Deliver insights like a team 10x your size," "Scale interviews without a human moderator"
3. **Centralization** — "One connected platform," "30+ native integrations," centralizes feedback from multiple sources
4. **Trustworthy, traceable AI** — "Every response includes point-level citations, so insights are transparent, traceable, and trustworthy" ← **key differentiator message in AI skepticism era**
5. **Democratization** — "Turn research into a company-wide conversation," "Be a strategic research provider to every team"
6. **Enterprise compliance** — SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 42001 (AI governance) — targets orgs with AI procurement scrutiny

## What They're NOT (Contra-Positioning)

- **Not a generic AI tool** — citations/traceability implicitly says "not ChatGPT on your data"

- **Not only for trained researchers** — "even for those without formal research training" (but careful not to alienate core buyer)

## UX Patterns to Learn From

> Marvin scores consistently high on ease-of-use. Specific patterns worth studying:

- **Frictionless import** — drag-and-drop any file, auto-transcribe, auto-tag immediately
- **Ask AI conversational query** — natural language across your entire corpus (not search filters)
- **Insight digests** — auto-push summaries to stakeholders without them needing to log in
- **Live observation rooms** — stakeholder participation without researcher hand-holding
- **Template-driven analysis** — guides users toward structured outputs, not blank canvas
- **Citation-linked outputs** — every AI claim traces back to source; builds trust immediately

## Threat Assessment

**Threat Level**: HIGH

**Why High**:
- Direct competitor for research + product buyers
- Strong brand with enterprise customers
- AI-moderated interviews directly overlaps our core capability
- Citations/traceability is a narrative we're building — they're already there

**Our Opportunity**:
- Their missing CRM/action layer is a real gap
- Cross-functional access beyond research teams is underdeveloped
- Real-time voice interviewer vs. their async AI interviews
- "Receipts" (verified evidence) is a stronger trust story than "citations"

**Watch For**:
- Adding CRM/account layer or integrations
- Self-serve paid tier launch
- Acquisitions in voice/real-time interview space
- Enterprise pricing changes
