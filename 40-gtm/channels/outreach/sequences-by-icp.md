# Outreach Sequences by ICP

> Draft sequences for the four primary ICPs. Principle: lead with *their* trigger, not our features; the demo/CTA is always "let me run your existing conversations and show you what's in them." Personalize the bracketed bits. Keep cold emails <90 words.

---

## A. Founder-led B2B seller  *(beachhead — warm/network-first)*
**Channel:** warm intros (StartupSD, Focus26, NCCC), then targeted LinkedIn. **Trigger:** doing their own sales, pile of un-mined calls.

**Email 1 — the lost-deal hook**
> Subject: who in your pipeline is actually ready to buy?
>
> Hey [name] — you're doing your own [discovery/demos] at [company], which means the answer to "who's ready to buy and who's about to go cold" is buried in calls you'll never re-listen to.
>
> I built UpSight to mine exactly that. Send me 10–20 of your recent call recordings and I'll send back a one-page read: expansion-ready accounts, churn risks, and the unmet needs — each with the quote that flagged it. No deck, just the findings.
>
> Worth a look?

**Email 2 (+3 days) — proof/value**
> Quick add: [founder peer / Cytodyme-type] had ~80 calls sitting in [Apollo/Zoom]. We surfaced 3 expansion-ready accounts they hadn't flagged. Took an afternoon. Want me to run yours?

**Email 3 (+5 days) — breakup**
> Last one — if mining your existing calls for "who's ready to buy" isn't a now-thing, no worries. I'll leave the offer open: 10 calls in, findings out, free.

## C. Apollo refugee  *(acquisition wedge — cost/trust/support, NOT CI depth)*
**Channel:** "Apollo alternatives" SEO/comparison page → retarget; G2/r/sales; LinkedIn complaint signals. **Trigger:** bounce disaster, credit bill-shock, renewal in 60 days, support failure.

**Email 1 — the switch hook**
> Subject: re: your Apollo renewal
>
> Saw you're [evaluating alternatives / frustrated with Apollo]. Most teams we talk to aren't leaving over features — it's the **data you can't trust** (bounces torching your sender rep), the **credit bills**, and **support that ghosts**.
>
> UpSight works on the conversation data you *already own* — turns it into "who's ready to buy / who's about to churn" — under $100/seat, no credits, real humans on support. Want a side-by-side on your actual numbers?

**Email 2 (+3 days)**
> The math most Apollo refugees miss: you're paying for a database that's ~1-in-3 wrong. We don't sell data — we make the conversations you've already had pay off. 15-min teardown of your setup?

> **⚠️ Do NOT** pitch "deeper meeting summaries" here — they don't churn over CI depth. Stay on trust/cost/support + "data you own."

## D. Pre-CRM / spreadsheet SMB  *(inbound-led; outbound is hard to target)*
**Channel:** mostly SEO ("Excel CRM template", "spreadsheet vs CRM", comparison pages) → email capture → nurture. Outbound only where firmographics hint at it (small B2B, no CRM in tech stack). **Trigger:** a deal/insight lost; a growth moment.

**Email 1 — the spreadsheet-pain hook**
> Subject: the deal your spreadsheet forgot
>
> If [company]'s relationships live in a spreadsheet, you already know the failure mode: someone goes cold, a follow-up slips, and you find out a month later.
>
> UpSight is the upgrade from the spreadsheet — not a heavy CRM. It remembers every conversation and tells you who's ready to buy and who's about to drop off. Works out of the box, under $100/seat. Want to see it on your own contacts?

**Email 2 (+4 days) — low-friction**
> No migration, no admin, no "implementation." Import your sheet, connect your calls, see the read in 10 minutes. Want a link?

> **⚠️** Anchored at $0 and low-urgency — manufacture urgency with a concrete near-loss, keep every step near-zero-friction.

## B. Boutique agency / consultancy  *(expansion — list-buildable)*
**Channel:** LinkedIn (Clutch/DesignRush firmographics), agency communities. **Trigger:** new client project; warm relationship went cold.

**Email 1 — the warm-relationship hook**
> Subject: the expansion accounts [agency] is leaving on the table
>
> 2–10 person shops like [agency] win most new revenue from clients you've already talked to — but those warm relationships go cold because there's no system holding the context.
>
> UpSight mines your discovery and client calls for repeat-buy and expansion signals, with the receipts to back a proposal. Under $100/seat, no admin. Want me to run it on a few recent client conversations?

**Email 2 (+4 days) — the deliverable angle**
> Bonus: when a client challenges a recommendation, you click → show the exact quote. "3 days of synthesis → 30 minutes." Useful on your next SOW?

## Sequence hygiene
- 3 touches max for cold; stop on reply.
- Every sequence's real CTA = **"let me run your existing conversations and show you what's in them"** (the receipts demo).
- Log experiments in `../../experiments/_index.md` (subject-line A/B, hook A/B). Replies/learnings → `../../../20-research/`.
