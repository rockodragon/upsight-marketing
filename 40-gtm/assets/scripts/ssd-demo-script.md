# SSD Demo Script — UpSight for Founders & Board Members
**Audience:** Startup San Diego — board members interviewing founders; transferable to founders recording customer meetings
**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover (record silent, narrate in post)
**Target length:** 2:30–3:00 min

---

## Scene Table

| #   | Scene                   | Duration | What to Show on Screen                                                                                      | Voiceover                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| --- | ----------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **The Problem**         | 0:20     | Blank notes app or messy Notion doc with bullet points from a "meeting"                                     | "You do 6 founder calls in a week. You take notes, maybe. A month later, you can't remember who said what — or what the pattern was."                                                              |
| 2   | **Record**              | 0:30     | Desktop app open → join a Zoom/Meet → hit Record → show live transcript appearing with speaker names        | "UpSight joins your meeting. The transcript appears in real time. You don't touch anything."                                                                                                       |
| 3   | **Interview Opens**     | 0:30     | Web app → interview detail → scroll to Evidence panel showing 3–5 AI-extracted highlights with exact quotes | "When the call ends, UpSight has already read it. Not a summary — specific claims, with the exact words that back them up. These are your receipts."                                               |
| 4   | **Themes Across Calls** | 0:40     | Themes page with 4–6 themes → click one → see 3 quotes from 3 different founders attributed by name         | "After 6 calls, patterns surface automatically. 'Enterprise sales cycles' appeared in 4 of them — here's exactly who said what. This is the synthesis memo you'd normally spend a Sunday writing." |
| 5   | **Person Profile**      | 0:20     | Click into a founder's People profile → name, company, interview history, key quotes                        | "Every person you've talked to has a profile. What they said, when, and which themes they're tied to."                                                                                             |
| 6   | **The Closer**          | 0:10     | Wide shot of Themes page or Journey Map — clean UI                                                          | "Board members run on pattern recognition. UpSight gives you receipts."                                                                                                                            |

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Project has 4–6 interviews already processed (not an empty project)
- [ ] Themes page shows 3+ themes each with 2+ evidence quotes
- [ ] At least one person profile with a clean name and interview history
- [ ] Desktop app installed and tested — do a 2-min test recording first
- [ ] Know which interview you'll use for Scene 3 (pick the best evidence)
- [ ] Name the project something clean for the demo (not "Test Project")

---

## Risk / Contingency

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Trigger.dev env vars broken → no evidence on new recording | Use a pre-seeded interview for Scenes 3–5; only show LIVE recording in Scene 2 |
| Desktop app crashes mid-record | Have a backup recording already done |
| Processing takes >5 min after Scene 2 | Pre-recorded interview for Scene 3 — cut directly to it |
| Live transcript doesn't appear in real time | Show the transcript that arrives post-call instead; mention "appears during or right after" |

---

## Voiceover Notes

- Record screen FIRST, silent
- Add voiceover in post (Descript, ScreenStudio, or just QuickTime + iMovie)
- Speak at 120 wpm — 300 words = 2:30
- Don't explain the UI — let it speak. Narrate the VALUE.
- End on "receipts" — that's the hook phrase for this audience
