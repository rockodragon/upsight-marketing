# Public Pricing Page Spec

## JTBD

When a founder-led seller, spreadsheet-run team, or consultant lands on our pricing page, help them quickly understand:

- what plan fits them
- what is included
- why the pricing feels fair
- what happens if they use more

The page should reduce purchase anxiety, not create it.

## Core message

**See who is warm, who is slipping, and what is blocking the next step.**

Team-friendly pricing. Metered on costly usage, not on every human who needs visibility.

## Pricing table

### Free — $0

Best for trying the workflow on real data.

- 1 workspace
- up to 2 members
- small monthly allowance
- hard cap on costly usage
- receipts, search, and basic evidence extraction

CTA: `Try it free`

### Starter — $39/mo

Best for a founder-led seller or tiny team.

- 1 workspace
- up to 3 members
- included monthly transcription / analysis allowance
- receipts, decision board, themes, lenses
- transparent overages or optional spend cap

CTA: `Start Starter`

### Team — $99/mo

Best for cofounder teams and small operators who need shared visibility.

- 3 workspaces
- up to 10 members
- larger included monthly allowance
- shared Today-page decision board
- exports and priority support

CTA: `Start Team`

### Consultant / agency expansion

Not a separate headline plan card. Explain it below the grid:

**Add extra client workspaces for $19-29 each.**

Reason: this matches how consultants expand value without forcing seat rebuys.

### Scale / API

Custom or contact sales.

- for agent builders
- for API-heavy usage
- for larger usage needs

CTA: `Talk to us`

## Copy blocks to include

### "How pricing works"

Short explainer:

Each paid plan includes a monthly allowance of the expensive work, like transcription and heavier AI analysis. If you use more, you can either upgrade, pay transparent overages, or set a spend cap.

### "What we do not charge for"

- inviting collaborators within your plan
- semantic search
- basic read access
- sharing the receipts

### "Why this model"

We price for the costly compute, not for every teammate who needs context. Small teams work better when sales, product, and ops can see the same evidence.

## FAQ

### Why not charge per seat?

Because the value increases when the right people can see the same evidence. We meter the expensive work, not the basic collaboration.

### What happens if I go over the included usage?

You can upgrade, pay transparent overages, or set a spend cap.

### Can consultants run multiple clients?

Yes. Team includes multiple workspaces, and consultants can add extra client workspaces.

### Are overages credits?

No. We should not use "credits" language on the public page. Explain the units plainly.

### What units should we show?

Prefer plain units like:

- transcription hours
- heavy analysis runs
- workspace count

Do not lead with abstract credit buckets.

## Visual / layout guidance

The page should emphasize:

1. the buyer outcome
2. plan fit
3. fairness and predictability

Not:

1. feature sprawl
2. AI jargon
3. credit gamification
