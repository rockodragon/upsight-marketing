# Research Benchmark Report — Template

> This is the **incentive** for both research-led tracks (we dropped cash — the report self-selects
> people genuinely invested in the space). It has two uses from one fill:
>
> 1. **Personalized send-back** (to each respondent) — honors the promise, includes their "how you
>    compare" line. **NO pitch.** This is the trust deposit.
> 2. **Gated public benchmark** (lead magnet) — same body, swap the personalized line for a soft CTA.
>
> Don't publish until **N ≥ ~20** responses (below that, say "early signal from N organizers/founders"
> and frame as preliminary). Anonymize every quote. Ship within the time-box you promised them.

---

## Fill rules
- **Honest math.** Report real N. If a stat is thin, label it "early signal."
- **Anonymize.** Quotes get role + segment only ("a founder running 2-person sales", "a corporate event organizer"). No names without explicit consent.
- **3–5 stats max.** A benchmark people actually read is short. One headline number, a few supporting.
- **The "how you compare" line is the magic.** It's why they open it. Generate per-respondent from their survey answers.

---

## Report body (template — fill the brackets)
