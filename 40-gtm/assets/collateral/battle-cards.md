# Battle Cards

> For demos, objection-handling, and switch campaigns. Each card: who they are → where they win → **where WE win** → the one-line wedge → traps to avoid. Honesty rule: where we lose, say so and disqualify gracefully.

**Our one-liner:** *Conversation intelligence + lightweight CRM that works on the data you already have and tells you who's ready to buy and who's about to churn — under $100/seat, no admin setup.*

---

## 🥊 Apollo  *(primary — the "refugee" wedge)*
- **What they are:** prospecting-database GTM platform ($150M ARR, 500K+ companies, $1.6B val) with bolted-on conversation intelligence. Per-seat ($49–149) + a credit system.
- **Where they win:** the prospecting-data moat (don't fight this), cheap all-in-one, self-serve.
- **Where WE win:** their CI is *insight without action* — generates next-steps as inert text and makes the user do the clerical work; never says "advanced/stalled/at-risk." Their **data is the real pain**: ~65% of users cite accuracy issues, 15–25% bounce rates, credit bill-shock, support widely rated worst-in-class.
- **Wedge:** *"Apollo finds the task and makes you do it. UpSight turns the conversation into the decision and the next action — and works on data you already own and trust, with real support, under $100/seat."*
- **⚠️ Trap:** don't sell "deeper CI" to Apollo refugees — they leave over **data/cost/support, not CI depth.** Lead with cost + trust + support + "works on data you own." Disqualify if they need a prospecting database (we're not that).
- **Switch hooks:** "Apollo renewal in 60 days?", deliverability disaster, surprise credit bill.

## 🥊 Gong / Chorus  *(down-market wedge)*
- **What they are:** best-in-class enterprise sales CI ($300M+ ARR), 250+ integrations, training program.
- **Where they win:** enterprise credibility, depth, integrations. **We lose enterprise — don't fight there.**
- **Where WE win:** price (<$100 vs $100–150+/seat, real cost $15K–40K), no admin/training, SMB-accessible, research-shaped not sales-only.
- **Wedge:** *"Gong is a $100K rollout with a training program. UpSight gives you the same conversation intelligence in an afternoon, under $100/seat, no admin."*
- **⚠️ Trap:** don't claim feature parity on enterprise sales coaching. Win on **accessibility + speed-to-value**, and on serving product *and* sales from one tool.

## 🥊 HubSpot / Salesforce  *(setup-friction wedge)*
- **What they are:** full CRM platforms; the "expensive siloed tool" anchor.
- **Where they win:** ecosystem, brand, breadth.
- **Where WE win:** overkill for small teams, require an admin/implementation, optimized for 20+ person orgs with established process.
- **Wedge:** *"Your CRM is a database you feed. UpSight is the intelligence that tells you who's ready to buy and who's about to churn — without an admin or an implementation project."*
- **⚠️ Trap:** we're not a CRM replacement for established teams. Position as the **intelligence layer** / the right first tool for pre-CRM teams.

## 🥊 Dovetail  *(simplicity + price wedge)*
- **What they are:** research-repository market leader ($700M val), enterprise-ready, 50+ integrations.
- **Where they win:** enterprise research orgs, integrations, brand in UXR.
- **Where WE win:** repository friction ("makes you a librarian"), AI-accuracy complaints, hidden enterprise pricing, analysis-only (no collection), too complex for non-researchers.
- **Wedge:** *"Dovetail makes you a librarian. UpSight makes you a decision — synthesis and action, no folder spelunking, transparent pricing."*
- **⚠️ Trap:** for a dedicated UXR team that already loves Dovetail, disqualify gracefully. We win the **non-researcher doing research** (founder/PM/consultant).

## 🥊 Attio  *(CI-depth wedge)*
- **What they are:** AI-native CRM, flexible data model, $116M raised, momentum.
- **Where they win:** CRM fundamentals, dev platform, "good-enough" call AI bundled in.
- **Where WE win:** conversation-analysis depth, evidence traceability with verbatim attribution, cross-conversation synthesis, non-sales/research use cases a CRM add-on can't reach.
- **Wedge:** *"Attio bundles good-enough call AI into a CRM. UpSight delivers research-grade evidence and cross-conversation decisions a CRM add-on can't."*

## 🥊 Nutshell  *(cross-conversation wedge)*
- **What they are:** real SMB CRM ($13–79), native Zoom/Meet/Teams recording, live support.
- **Where they win:** actual pipeline/CRM, native recording, simple, cheap.
- **Where WE win:** depth — they do single-meeting summaries; we connect dots **across every conversation** (themes, lenses, evidence) and serve product *and* sales.
- **Wedge:** *"Nutshell summarizes each meeting. UpSight connects the dots across every conversation and tells you what to do next."*

## 🥊 Spreadsheets  *(the real beachhead competitor)*
- **What they are:** free, zero-friction, universal, no lock-in. **Inertia is the #1 competitor.**
- **Where they win:** free; chosen *because* real tools felt heavy.
- **Where WE win:** everything past ~50 contacts — no synthesis, no cross-conversation pattern, no "who's ready/at-risk," manual entry dies in weeks, things slip.
- **Wedge:** *"Your spreadsheet remembers what was said. It doesn't tell you who's about to buy or churn. UpSight does — one tool, no setup."*
- **⚠️ Trap:** they're anchored at $0 and low-urgency. **Manufacture urgency** ("here's the deal you'd have lost") and stay ruthlessly low-friction.
