## Competitive Position

### Their Strengths

1. **Security**: UK MoD accreditation, OFFICIAL-SENSITIVE
2. **Government**: Strong public sector presence
3. **Pricing Model**: Transparent, flexible licensing
4. **Multi-agency**: Built for cross-org collaboration
5. **G-Cloud**: Easy government procurement

### Their Weaknesses

1. **Generic Collaboration**: Not consulting-specific
2. **No Voice/AI**: No interview or analysis features
3. **Not Stakeholder-Focused**: General collaboration tool
4. **UK-Centric**: Primarily UK government market
5. **No Deliverables**: Doesn't generate reports

## How We Compete

### Direct Comparison

| Feature | Kahootz | UpSight for Consulting |
|---------|---------|------------------------|
| Secure collaboration | Yes, excellent | Basic |
| Document sharing | Yes | Yes |
| Voice capture | No | Yes |
| AI analysis | No | Yes |
| Stakeholder focus | Partial | Yes |
| Consulting workflows | No | Yes |
| Security certs | MoD-level | Building |

### Positioning Difference
- **Kahootz**: Secure workspace for multi-org collaboration
- **UpSight**: Interview-to-insight-to-deliverable for consultants

*Kahootz is a collaboration platform, not a stakeholder engagement or consulting tool.*

## Threat Assessment

**Threat Level**: VERY LOW

**Why Very Low**:
- Different category entirely (collaboration vs. interview analysis)
- No voice/AI roadmap visible
- Government/enterprise focus, not SMB consulting
- Would require significant pivot to compete

**Watch For**:
- Acquisition of stakeholder/interview tools
- AI/voice feature announcements
