- UpSight: Discovery interviews → Recommendations
- OCMS: Implement change → Track adoption

## Consulting Relevance

### Where OCMS Fits in Consulting
1. **After Discovery**: When implementing recommendations
2. **Change Projects**: Digital transformation, M&A integration

### Where UpSight Fits

3. **Deliverable Creation**: SOW, requirements, reports

## Threat Assessment

**Threat Level**: VERY LOW

**Why Very Low**:
- Different phase of consulting engagement
- Change management ≠ discovery/research
- No voice/interview direction visible
- Would require fundamental pivot to compete

**Complementary Opportunity**:
- UpSight for discovery → OCMS for implementation
- Potential integration partner, not competitor

**Watch For**:
- Discovery/interview features
- AI synthesis capabilities
- Consulting-specific positioning
