# Attio

**URL:** attio.com
**Category:** AI-Native CRM / Go-to-Market Platform
**Threat Level:** Medium
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-26

## Overview

Attio is a London-based, AI-native CRM founded in 2017 that combines the flexibility of a database/spreadsheet with relationship intelligence and embedded AI. It positions itself as the modern replacement for legacy CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot, targeting startups, VC firms, and growth-stage GTM teams. With 5,000 customers and $116M in total funding, it is one of the fastest-growing new CRM platforms and recently expanded into conversation intelligence with its Call Intelligence feature.

## Positioning

Attio describes itself as "a new vision for CRM" and "the first AI-native CRM for go-to-market builders." Core messaging centers on: (1) hyper-flexible data model where custom objects and attributes let teams model any business process, (2) automatic relationship intelligence enriching records from email, calendar, and external data without manual entry, and (3) embedded AI that turns unstructured data into structured, actionable fields. They lean heavily on a "Notion meets Salesforce" analogy.

## Key Features

- **Custom Objects & Flexible Data Model** -- Build any data structure beyond standard contacts/companies
- **Relationship Intelligence** -- Auto-enrichment from email, calendar, and external data; maps stakeholders and company hierarchies
- **AI Attributes** -- AI-powered fields that auto-summarize notes, classify data, generate structured values from unstructured inputs
- **Call Intelligence** -- Native call recording (Zoom, Meet, Teams) with real-time transcription, buying signal detection, customizable insight templates
- **AI Workflows** -- Multi-step automations with AI-powered triggers
- **AI Agent Integrations** -- MCP server for Claude/ChatGPT; read/write CRM access from external AI agents
- **Developer Platform & App SDK** -- Build custom apps in TypeScript/React; App Store marketplace launched Dec 2025
- **Real-time Collaboration** -- Simultaneous editing, live updates, multiplayer workspace
- **Reporting & Analytics** -- Built-in dashboards with customizable views

## Target Customers

- **Primary:** Startups and scale-ups (seed through Series C) who find HubSpot/Salesforce too rigid or expensive
- **Strong niche:** Venture capital and private equity firms (deal flow, LP tracking, portfolio management)
- **Secondary:** Modern GTM teams at mid-market companies; agencies; creator economy businesses
- **Notable customers:** Lovable, Granola, Modal, Replicate, Public.com

Attio does NOT specifically target consultants, product managers doing customer research, or small qualitative research teams.

## UpSight vs Attio

### Where they overlap

- Both record and transcribe customer conversations, then use AI to extract structured insights
- Both maintain contact and organization databases enriched by conversation data
- Both market themselves as AI-first rather than AI-bolted-on
- Both target teams that find enterprise CRMs overkill

### Where UpSight wins

- **Depth of conversation analysis:** Conversation lenses, evidence "receipts" with verbatim attribution, empathy maps, and multi-framework extraction go far deeper than Attio's call summaries
- **Research-grade insight synthesis:** Themes, cross-interview patterns, persona clustering, ICP scoring -- Attio has no equivalent
- **Non-sales use cases:** Customer discovery, product research, user testing, consulting workflows Attio doesn't address
- **Evidence traceability:** Every insight links to a specific moment in a specific conversation. Attio's AI outputs lack rigorous source attribution
- **Qualitative research toolkit:** Surveys (Ask links), interview guides, persona generation, journey mapping -- Attio has no version of this

### Where Attio wins

- **CRM fundamentals:** Pipeline management, deal tracking, email sequences, workflow automations
- **Data model flexibility:** Custom objects for modeling any business process
- **Integrations & ecosystem:** Developer platform, App SDK, App Store, MCP server
- **Scale & momentum:** $116M raised, 115 employees, 5,000 customers, 4x ARR growth
- **Real-time collaboration:** Multiplayer editing and live workspace updates
- **Data enrichment:** Automatic company/contact enrichment from external sources

### Key risk

Attio's Call Intelligence could expand into deeper conversation analysis. However, the bigger risk is that prospects choose Attio because it bundles "good enough" conversation AI with a full CRM. Counter-positioning: UpSight delivers research-grade depth that a CRM add-on cannot match -- evidence attribution, cross-conversation synthesis, and qualitative research workflows that Attio's architecture is not designed for.
