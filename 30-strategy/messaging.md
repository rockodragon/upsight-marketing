# Messaging

> Source of truth for how UpSight talks about itself. Pull from `00-control/north-star.md`.

## One-liner
UpSight gives distributed teams **receipts for every customer insight** — so no signal gets lost between conversations.

## Three-line version
Generic CRMs capture deals. Transcription tools capture words. Neither captures truth.
UpSight is the memory layer for customer conversations: structured evidence, themes, and personas that compound over time.
Built for founders and product teams running active customer discovery.

## Key concepts (use these phrases)
- **Receipts for every customer insight**
- **Memory/accumulation layer** — not single-call analysis
- **Chief-of-staff vibe** for distributed teams
- **Trust recession** (Shayna Davis frame) — why generic outreach fails now

## What we're not
- Not a transcription tool (transcription is table stakes)
- Not a generic CRM (we're underneath the CRM, not replacing it)
- Not single-call LLM analysis (we accumulate)

## Audience-specific framing

### To founders
"Stop losing customer truth between calls. UpSight builds the memory."

### To product teams
"Every customer conversation, structured. Themes that survive the org chart."

### To people sick of Apollo / generic outreach
"Apollo finds emails. UpSight finds the conversation worth having."

## Voice
- Confident, not precious
- Specific, not abstract — name the pain in concrete terms
- Founder-to-founder, not vendor-to-buyer
- Faith-adjacent OK in personal channels (Crossboard context); kept neutral in product channels
