# Messaging by ICP

> Per-ICP messaging derived from the candidate-ICP brief (`README.md`). Core brand messaging lives in `../messaging.md`; this maps the core promise onto each segment's trigger and pain. Use for landing pages, ads, outreach (`../../40-gtm/channels/outreach/`), and demo talk-tracks.

## Core promise (all segments)
**"Show me who is warm, who is slipping, and what is blocking the next step."** Conversation intelligence + lightweight CRM that works on the data you already have and outputs decisions, not dashboards. Team-friendly, no admin-heavy setup.

Master positioning frame: **between sheets and silos** — past the spreadsheet, under the expensive siloed tools.

## Message hierarchy (what to say first)
1. **Trigger / pain** in their words (above) — earn the read.
2. **The decision we deliver** — "who is warm / who is slipping / what is blocked," not a feature list.
3. **The receipts** — every claim links to the verbatim quote. *"Anyone can summarize. Few can prove."*
4. **Low friction** — works on data you already have, team-friendly pricing, no admin.

## What NOT to say (per segment)
- **Apollo refugees:** don't lead with "deeper conversation intelligence" — they churn over data/cost/support, not CI depth.
- **Spreadsheet users:** don't say "CRM" first (it's why they avoided tools) — say "upgrade from your spreadsheet."
- **Anyone SMB:** don't compare to Gong on enterprise sales coaching — compare on speed-to-value and price.
- **Dedicated UX researchers:** don't pitch against Dovetail head-on — we win the *non-researcher doing research.*
