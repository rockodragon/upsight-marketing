# Weekly review

> Sunday evening or Monday morning. 30-45 minutes. Same time every week.
> Calendar-blocked on rickmoy@gmail.com AND rick@deeplight.digital.

---

## The ritual (in order — don't skip steps)

### 1. Read last week's `00-control/status.md` (5 min)
- What actually shipped?
- What slipped, and why?
- What surprised you?

### 2. **Sales pipeline review** (10 min) — *most important section*
Open `00-control/pipeline.md`.
- For every active deal: what's the stage, what's the next action, who owns it?
- Anything stuck >1 week without movement? Decide: push, deprioritize, or kill.
- What did this week's sales conversations teach you? Update `30-research/evidence-log.md`.
- **Output:** every active deal has a clear next action with a date.

### 3. Research pulse (5 min)
- Refresh `30-research/themes.md` from UpSight MCP.
- New evidence in `evidence-log.md`?
- Any theme strong enough to change priorities or messaging?

### 4. Backlog scan (5 min)
- Glance at `00-control/backlog.md`.
- Anything that should be promoted because of new evidence or pipeline movement?
- Anything that should be killed?

### 5. Rewrite `00-control/priorities.md` (10 min)
- Top 5 only.
- **At least 2 must be `#gtm`.** Non-negotiable.
- **If a sales deal needs unblocking, it's #1.** Always.
- Time-box stuck items.
- Move unpicked items to `backlog.md` (not parking lot — long-term).

### 6. Kill list (5 min)
- What am I explicitly NOT doing this week?
- Log in `priorities.md` "Killed this week" + mirror to `decisions.md`.

---

## Daily mini-rituals (don't put in calendar — just do them)

**Morning (2 min):** Open `priorities.md`. That's the day's filter.
**End of day (5 min):** Append to `00-control/status.md` + update `20-gtm/pipeline.md`.
